Tomás S. Grigera

University of La Plata

I am a researcher working at the Statistical Biophysics (BiEs) group at IFLySiB, a research institute jointly dependent from CONICET (Argentina’s national research council) and the University of La Plata, and staffed mainly by physicists. I also teach various courses at the Department of physics of the same University (about 2km from IFLYSIB).

I graduated in physics at La Plata and obtained my PhD here in 1998. Before joining IFLYSIB in late 2014, I worked at INIFTA as a CONICET researcher from late 2003, and before that I was a postdoc in the Department of Physics of Northeastern University, Boston (1998–1999) and in the Department of Physics of the University of Rome La Sapienza (2000–2003).
