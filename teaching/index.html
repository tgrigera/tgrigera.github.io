Teaching

I normally teach two undergraduate courses per year at the Departament of Physics of UNLP. Depending on the year I teach an extra graduate course, sometimes in collaboration with other professors.

I teach or have taught, at the undergraduate level:

  • Statistical mechanics (2020–2022)

    The advanced StatMech undergrad course (taken in the last year of physics, after passing a one-semester introductory course)

  • Computer simulations (2018–2020)

  • Freshman physics (2015–2017)

  • Computing (2013–2016)

  • Partial differential equations and probability (2012–2014)

  • Thermodynamics (2009–2011)

  • Physics for chemstry and biochemistry freshman year (2005–2011)

Graduate courses:

  • Classical Mechancis (2017–2019)

    A course part of a Master for physics teachers

  • Physics of disordered systems (2009, 2012, 2014)

  • Order and disorder in complex systems (2017)

More details on the courses are given in the Spanish page.
